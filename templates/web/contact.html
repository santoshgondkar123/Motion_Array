Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about MotionHive

How do I download templates after subscription?
After subscribing, you'll get unlimited access to all 1000+ templates. Simply browse any template and click the "Download" button. Your downloads will start immediately.
What software do I need for these templates?
Our templates work with Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Final Cut Pro, and other major video editing software.
Can I use templates for commercial projects?
Yes! All templates come with a commercial license. You can use them in client projects, YouTube videos, commercials, and any commercial work.
How often are new templates added?
We add 50+ new motion graphics templates every week. Subscribe to stay updated with the latest trends and styles.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. No questions asked, and you keep access until the billing period ends.
Do you offer custom motion design services?
Yes! We have a team of professional motion designers available for custom projects. Contact our sales team for a quote.
